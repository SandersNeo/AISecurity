# AI Security Governance

> **Module 07: Policy, Compliance, and Enterprise Security**

---

## Overview

Governance provides the organizational framework for AI security. This module covers policy development, audit procedures, compliance requirements, and risk assessment for AI systems in enterprise environments.

---

## Why Governance Matters

| Challenge | Need |
|-----------|------|
| Regulatory compliance | GDPR, AI Act, industry standards |
| Liability management | Clear policies and procedures |
| Consistent protection | Enterprise-wide standards |
| Incident response | Defined procedures |
| Stakeholder trust | Demonstrable security |

## Lessons Overview

### Policy Development
**Time:** 40 minutes | **Difficulty:** Intermediate

- Policy lifecycle management
- Stakeholder alignment
- Policy templates
- Update procedures

### Risk Assessment
**Time:** 45 minutes | **Difficulty:** Intermediate

- AI-specific risk factors
- Impact assessment
- Risk matrices
- Mitigation planning

### Compliance Mapping
**Time:** 45 minutes | **Difficulty:** Advanced

- GDPR implications for AI
- EU AI Act requirements
- Industry standards (NIST, ISO)
- Cross-jurisdictional challenges

### Audit Procedures
**Time:** 40 minutes | **Difficulty:** Intermediate

- Audit program design
- Evidence collection
- Finding classification
- Remediation tracking
